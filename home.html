The Mathematics for Biology consortium is a research initiative based at Université Côte d’Azur, bringing together mathematicians and biologists to address complex challenges in the life sciences. By fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, the consortium aims to develop innovative mathematical models and tools to better understand biological systems and processes.
